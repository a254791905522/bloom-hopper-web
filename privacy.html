Bloom Hopper Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 14, 2026

Bloom Hopper is an offline single-player iOS game. This policy explains how the game handles information.

No Personal Information Collected

Bloom Hopper does not collect, store, transmit, sell, or share personal information.

Local Progress

The game may store gameplay progress, settings, stars, unlocked levels, and high scores locally on your device. This information stays on your device and is not transmitted to us.

No Tracking, Ads, or Analytics

Bloom Hopper does not include advertising SDKs, analytics SDKs, tracking technologies, social login, account systems, or third-party purchase SDKs.

No Network Requirement

The game is designed to work offline and does not require an internet connection for gameplay.

Children's Privacy

Bloom Hopper is suitable for all ages. Because the game does not collect personal information, it does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Contact

If you have questions about this policy, please contact support through the App Store product page for Bloom Hopper.

Changes to This Policy

If the game changes in a way that affects privacy, this policy will be updated accordingly.
